## 3 Philosophical Pillars (with citations)

### A. Neural Signal Maximization
- **Mechanism**: Maximal strength → enhanced corticospinal excitability + rate coding
- **Citations**: Behm & Sale (1993); Haff & Triplett (2016); Sidhu et al. (2009)
- **Logic**: Max strength = ceiling for power expression. ไม่ใช่ "ใหญ่กว่า = แข็งกว่า" แต่เป็น "neural drive ที่แม่นกว่า"

### B. Technique as Rate-Limiting Factor (Funnel Analogy)
- **Coach quote**: *"strength development is like filling a funnel; technique determines the exit aperture"*
- **Mechanism**: Constraint-led motor learning (Newell, 1986). ระหว่าง strength phase ต้องรักษา motor "attractor states" (Schöner & Kelso, 1988) ไม่ให้ stroke แตก
- **Practical**: ฝึก swim ที่ stroke rate 2-3% ใต้ race pace (~58 sr vs 60-63 race) ระหว่าง strength block

### C. Sequential Periodization (Phase-Dependent)
- **ห้าม**: Concurrent maximal strength + maximal swimming ใน microcycle เดียวกัน
- **Mechanism**: AMPK vs mTOR pathway interference (Coffey & Hawley, 2007); SFRA dynamics (Zatsiorsky & Kraemer, 2006)
- **Approach**: Block periodization แท้ ๆ (Issurin, 2008) — ลำดับ stimulus, ไม่ใช่ผสมกันทุกสัปดาห์

## 3-Phase Periodization Model (Novel Detail)

### Phase 1: General Strength (16-20 wk pre-comp)

| Component | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| Swim freq | 2-3x/wk |
| Swim intensity | RPE 7-8 (submaximal) |
| Stroke rate | ~58 (vs race 60-63) |
| Resistance | Maximal strength 1-5 RM (pull-ups, squats, Olympic derivatives) |
| Mobility | Daily, extensive |

### Phase 2: Strength-Power Transition (4-6 wk)

| Component | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| Swim freq | 4-5x/wk |
| Resisted sprint | 20m × 4-5 reps × 2x/wk |
| Resistance | Reduced volume; velocity emphasis |
| Method | **French Contrast** — heavy resistance → resisted swim → bodyweight swim |
| Logic | Complex training exploiting Post-Activation Potentiation (PAP) |

### Phase 3: Specific Preparation (16-20 wk)

| Component | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| Swim | Primary focus; race modeling |
| Resistance | Maintenance only — minimal fatigue spillover |
| Progression | Accumulate sprint volume @ 95-100% race pace |
| Taper | Volume + intensity reduction |
| Frequency | 4-6x/wk |

## Critique of Traditional Methods (especially USRPT)

| Factor | USRPT | McEvoy |
|--------|-------|--------|
| Periodization | Minimal, year-round consistent | Radical phase-dependent shifts |
| Volume logic | Density (high freq @ race pace) | Intensity-concentration (min vol, max quality) |
| Equipment | Excluded | Integrated (parachutes, 1080 Sprint, fins) |
| Strength training | Excluded | Extensive (max strength primary) |
| Session ending | Volume target | Quality degradation termination |
| Recovery | Minimal gaps | 24-48 hr in strength phase |

**Article challenges**: ทุก assumption ที่ว่า "anaerobic capacity ต้อง substrate-prep ผ่าน high-volume lactate work". Slow glycolytic / oxidative capacity → article อ้างว่า **ไม่เกี่ยวกับ 50m** เลย

## Athlete Selection Criteria

**ใช้ได้ ✅**:
- Established technical proficiency (stroke patterns นิ่งแล้ว)
- High strength trainability (neural responder > hypertrophy responder)
- Autonomous motivation (เหมาะกับ self-coaching)
- 50m/100m specialist
- Mature training age

**ไม่แนะนำ ❌**:
- Junior swimmers (ต้อง high-frequency tech work)
- คนที่ strength base ยังไม่มี
- คนที่ต้องการ coach supervision สูง
