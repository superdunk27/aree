## ⚡ The new 80/20 (Round 3 — เพิ่มจาก Round 2)

### 1. Track cycling sprint — closest LAND analog ที่ no-impact
- **Strength benchmark**: elite track sprint cyclist squat 2.5-3× BW (Hoy 2.6, Förstemann 2.9, Lavreysen ~2.3)
- **Lavreysen lesson**: GOAT ปัจจุบันมี squat-to-BW *ต่ำกว่า* คนรุ่นก่อน — เก่งที่ mechanical efficiency, ไม่ใช่ raw strength
- **Plateau insight**: สำหรับ swim, past **1.75-2× BW** (squat or trap bar DL) ROI ลดลง. ขั้นต่อไป = velocity, ไม่ใช่ load
- **Gate start mechanics** = block start analog. Standing start = wall push-off analog
- **Peak power testing**: Wattbike, isokinetic — track สำหรับ swim มี analog (semi-tethered F-V test from Round 2 #02)
- **British Cycling periodization**: 4-yr Olympic cycle, peak ใน 6-week blocks
- **Zero-impact = สำคัญสำหรับ Toey age 31 returning** — track cycling = ergometer ที่ดีกว่า hill sprint สำหรับ masters returnee

### 2. Kayak K1 200m — closest WATER analog (33-40s, fluid resistance)
- **Lum & Barbosa IST protocol**: isometric prone bench pull at **120° elbow angle**
  - +13.9% on 200m TT (d=0.45) vs +5.8% dynamic-only (d=0.18). Same volume, different angle/intent
  - **120° = matches both kayak catch AND swim EVF (early vertical forearm)**
- **Footrest force** in kayak = elite vs sub-elite differ by **3-26×** — boat is pulled by legs through footrest, ไม่ใช่ arms อย่างเดียว
- **Direct swim translation**: hip-driven freestyle closes the same kinetic chain — leg drive feeds the catch
- **Tethered/leashed paddling** = direct analog ของ in-pool semi-tethered F-V test (Round 2 #02)
- **Posterior chain emphasis**: lats, rhomboids, lower back, hip flexors — exactly swim sprint
- **Hungarian/German programs lead** — internals poorly documented in English peer-review

### 3. Free diving — สำหรับ 0-1 breath 50m specifically
- **Limiter** = CO2 tolerance + dive reflex underdevelopment, **NOT lung capacity**
- **CO2 tolerance table** (start protocol): 8 holds × 1:00 hold / 2:30 rest, **ลด rest 15s ทุก hold** จนเหลือ 0:30
- **O2 tolerance table**: fixed rest, **เพิ่ม hold duration** 15s ทุก rep
- **Dive reflex** = bradycardia (HR -20-30%), peripheral vasoconstriction, splenic contraction (RBC release ~3-5%)
- **Splenic adaptation key insight**: Schagatay 8-wk study → baseline volume +25%, แต่ acute contraction per hold ไม่เพิ่ม
  - แปลว่า splenic O2 boost ได้จาก **serial pre-race breath-holds ใน warmup** ไม่ใช่ "หายใจลึก ๆ ครั้งเดียว"
- **Robertson 2021**: 2 weeks dynamic apnea → 50m time 25.51→24.64s. Real, fast, replicable
- **Adaptation timeline**: 2-4 wk = noticeable, 8-12 wk = stable
- **🚨 SAFETY (สำคัญมาก)**:
  - Shallow Water Blackout (SWB) คือสาเหตุการเสียชีวิตของ trained swimmer/freediver
  - **ห้าม hyperventilation pre-hold** (กดความรู้สึกอยากหายใจโดยไม่เพิ่ม O2)
  - **ห้ามฝึก breath-hold คนเดียวในน้ำ** ทุกกรณี
  - **ห้ามเด็ก, depression history, cardiac issues**

### 4. Hand-balancing / GST — mobility-as-strength
- **Honest reality check**: loaded-mobility effect size ~0.54 (modest), plateau ที่ 6-8 weeks
- **Adult mobility ปิด gap ได้ ~30-50% ใน 8 weeks**, ไม่ใช่ 100%. Smith-McEvoy "5.6→5.16s ดิ่ง" คือ n=1 + confounded
- **Defensible framing**: mobility = **durability investment** ไม่ใช่ performance magic
- **Top methodologies**:
  - Christopher Sommer (GymnasticBodies / GST Foundation series)
  - Yuri Marmerstein (hand-balancing for adults, wrist conditioning protocols)
  - Emmet Louis (loaded stretching, end-range strengthening)
  - Ido Portal (overhyped marketing, but movement methodology has merit)
- **Loaded mobility (EQI) > static stretching** for permanent ROM gain (Behm/Chaouachi research)
- **Swim-relevant gymnastic exercises**:
  - **Jefferson curl** — thoracic flexion under load (streamline, dive)
  - **German hang** — shoulder posterior + scapular control
  - **Side splits progression** — hip mobility (kick efficiency)
  - **Dragon squat** — hip + ankle mobility
  - **Wall-supported handstand** — overhead shoulder + scapular upward rotation (catch position)
  - **Cossack squat** — single-leg + groin (turn push-off)
- **Wrist conditioning เป็น must** ก่อน hand-balance work (Marmerstein protocol, ~6-8 wk progression)
- **Sample weekly**: 2-3 sessions × 30-45 min, integrable with swim/strength

```
- LOW days: hand-balance practice (wall-supported, wrist conditioning first)
- Pre-race: serial breath-hold sequence to prime splenic O2 boost

Phase progression (12 weeks):
- Phase 1 (Wk 1-4): mobility base (GST), wrist conditioning, dry CO2 tables, base strength
- Phase 2 (Wk 5-8): integrate isometrics (Lum 120°), increase plyo, dynamic apnea begins
- Phase 3 (Wk 9-12): peak power, contrast methods, race-specific breath protocols

Safety gates:
- ห้าม dynamic apnea solo. Always with spotter.
- ห้าม depth jump without 1.5× BW squat baseline.
- ห้าม high CO2 tables ใน initial 2 weeks (adaptation needed).
- ห้าม Olympic lift derivatives without coaching.
```

## 🚨 Round 3 specific red flags

14. **Hyperventilation before breath-hold** — masks CO2 signal, ไม่เพิ่ม O2 → shallow water blackout
15. **Breath-hold training alone** — never. Spotter required.
16. **Hand-balance without wrist prep** — wrist injury common in adult onset
17. **Trying to match Lavreysen's squat number** — wrong mental model. Past 2× BW for swim, velocity > load
18. **Mobility chasing as primary intervention** — ROI plateaus at 8 weeks. Use as durability layer, ไม่ใช่ main lever
19. **Copying McEvoy's program directly** — n=1, idiosyncratic, multiple confounded changes
20. **Ido Portal mystique** — methodology has merit, marketing/cult layer is empty calories

| Claim | Evidence | Caveat |
|------|----------|--------|
| Track cycling F-V methods transfer to swim | Mechanistic strong | No direct cross-sport RCT |
| 1.75-2× BW squat is plateau for swim | Inferred from track cycling + McEvoy | Specific cutoff unverified |

| Footrest force critical in kayak (3-26× elite vs sub-elite) | Strong | Real RCT data |
| CO2 tolerance is the limiter for 50m breath-holding | Strong | Replicated multiple sport contexts |
